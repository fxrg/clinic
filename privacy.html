1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide you with quality healthcare services and manage your appointments efficiently. The types of information we collect include:

  • Personal Information: Full name, email address, and account credentials when you register for an account.
  • Appointment Data: Doctor selection, appointment dates and times, specialty information, and any notes you provide about your symptoms or visit reason.
  • Usage Data: Browser type, pages visited, and interaction patterns to improve our service quality and user experience.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and screen resolution to ensure proper display and responsive design.
info

We never collect sensitive financial information such as credit card numbers or banking details through our appointment system.

analytics
2. How We Use Your Data

Your personal data is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • Appointment Management: Scheduling, confirming, rescheduling, and tracking your medical appointments with our specialists.
  • Account Authentication: Verifying your identity to protect access to your personal health records and booking history.
  • Communication: Sending appointment reminders, confirmations, and important updates about your scheduled visits.
  • Service Improvement: Analyzing anonymized usage patterns to enhance our platform's usability, performance, and features.
  • Compliance: Meeting legal and regulatory obligations related to healthcare data management.

lock
3. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal and medical information:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard TLS/SSL protocols.
  • Password Protection: User passwords are hashed using bcrypt algorithm and are never stored in plain text.
  • Access Control: Strict role-based access ensures only authorized personnel can view sensitive patient data.
  • Regular Audits: Our systems undergo periodic security audits and vulnerability assessments by university IT security teams.
  • Local Storage: When using the offline-capable version, your data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted externally.
verified_user

Our security infrastructure meets university-grade encryption standards, ensuring your clinical data is protected at the highest institutional level.

cookie
4. Cookies & Tracking

Our application uses minimal browser storage to enhance your experience:

  • Session Storage: We use localStorage to maintain your login session and store appointment data for seamless interaction across page visits.
  • Essential Cookies: Session cookies are used to authenticate your identity and maintain secure access to protected pages.
  • No Third-Party Tracking: We do not use third-party analytics, advertising trackers, or social media tracking pixels on our platform.

You can clear your browser's local storage at any time through your browser settings, which will sign you out and remove locally stored appointment data.

share
5. Data Sharing

We are committed to keeping your data private. Your information may be shared only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Healthcare Providers: Appointment details are shared with the specific doctor you have booked to ensure they are prepared for your visit.
  • University Administration: Anonymized, aggregated data may be used for institutional reporting and quality improvement purposes.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or governmental regulation.
block

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal or medical information to third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

gavel
6. Your Rights

As a patient and user of Clinic Scholar, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can view all your stored data including appointment records and personal information through your account dashboard.
  • Correction: You have the right to request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete personal information.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting our support team.
  • Portability: You may request a copy of your data in a standard, machine-readable format.
  • Withdrawal: You can withdraw consent for data processing at any time, which may affect your ability to use certain features.

schedule
7. Data Retention

We retain your data for the following periods:

  • Active Accounts: Personal information and appointment history are retained as long as your account remains active.
  • Inactive Accounts: Accounts inactive for more than 24 months may be flagged for deletion with prior notification.
  • Completed Appointments: Historical appointment records are maintained for a minimum of 5 years for medical record-keeping compliance.
  • Post-Deletion: After account deletion, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.
